Ukuhub

UI/UX Design

  • Brief

    Ukuhub is a conceptual smartphone app that serves as a central hub for all ukulele resources. It's a platform where ukulele enthusiasts of all levels can learn or practice the basics of the instrument with tools such as the chords chart and tuner. They can also create and save song tab playlists with their favourite tunes to strum along to.

  • Collaborators

    Cindy Cheng, Linda Nguyen

  • Role

    UI/UX Designer

  • Browse song tabs/tutorials in Discovery [1/6]

  • Save added resources into new or existing folders [2/6]

  • Look up chord positions and hear how they sound [3/6]

  • Learn and practice different strumming patterns [4/6]

  • A handy tuner to make sure the ukulele is on point [5/6]

  • Menu of the app's different features and functions [6/6]

The tools and functionalities of the Ukuhub app eliminates the need for ukulele players to have to download multiple apps or bookmark numerous websites in order to refer back to their resources (tuner, song tabs, chords chart, and more). The need for the different features of the app came from interview and survey responses from new and experienced ukulele players.
