Wiivv Purchase Kits

Packaging Design

  • Client

    Wiivv creates 3D-printed insoles that are custom-fit to every foot and are designed to relieve foot pain. Insoles are made to order straight from the smartphone app.

  • Role

    Visual Designer

  • Brief

    I collaborated with the Marketing and Design teams to come up with a creative solution for Wiivv's retail packaging. Each kit includes a redeemable purchase code for one pair of insoles and a measurement guide to aid the foot measurement process on the app.

  • Front cover of the Purchase Kit [1/5]

  • Back cover of the Purchase Kit [2/5]

  • Purchase Kit contents [3/5]

  • Info Card [4/5]

  • Measurement Guide [5/5]

After going through numerous rounds of testing and feedback, the final design is shown above. The challenge with this project was to find a way to clearly show on the front side of the package that the product is a pair of insoles. The final design displays top and bottom views of the product, as well as some iconography to describe the length of the insole and the types of shoes that it is recommended for.
